原文: Background findings were observed in several organs throughout the dose groups including the control group. A minimal decrease in glycogen content in the liver at the high dose animals compared to the control animals is considered within the range of physiological variation of glycogen content in the liver. The slight tubular atrophy/hypospermatogenesis in the testes of the high dose male dog is also considered within the physiological range for animals at this age, as the testes are not yet fully mature. The male high dose animal also showed minimally decreased cell content in the periarterial sheath of the spleen as a single event.
译文: 在各个剂量组（包括对照组）的几个器官中观察到了背景发现。与对照组动物相比，高剂量组动物的肝糖原含量轻微减少，认为在肝糖原含量的生理变化范围内。高剂量雄性犬的睾丸中轻微的管状萎缩/少精症也被认为在该年龄动物的生理范围内，因为睾丸尚未完全成熟。高剂量雄性动物还显示脾脏动脉周围鞘区细胞含量轻微减少，作为单一事件出现。
--------------------------------------------------
原文: 	2.7	Toxicokinetic Data
译文: 	2.7	毒代动力学数据
--------------------------------------------------
原文: The results of the toxicokinetic evaluation at steady-state are summarized in Table 2-3. The exposure in all plasma samples of the vehicle groups (0 mg/kg) was below the lower limit of quantification (LLOQ = 3 µg/L).
译文: 稳态毒代动力学评估的结果总结在表2-3中。载体组（0 mg/kg）所有血浆样本中的暴露量均低于定量下限（LLOQ = 3 µg/L）。
--------------------------------------------------
原文: Exposures of BAY 2469430 were generally increased with dose in both genders. Only Cmax at 80 mg/kg in females and AUC(0-24) at 20 mg/kg in males showed a tendency for subproportional increase with dose. Except from a moderate increase in AUC(0-24) at 20 mg/kg in females, no relevant differences in exposure were observed between the two sexes.
译文: BAY 2469430在男女两性中暴露量总体上随着剂量的增加而增加。只有女性在80 mg/kg时的Cmax和男性在20 mg/kg时的AUC(0-24)显示出剂量不成比例增加的倾向。除女性在20 mg/kg时AUC(0-24)中等程度的增加外，在两性之间未观察到任何相关的暴露差异。
--------------------------------------------------
原文: Both genders did not show any relevant accumulation of Cmax or AUC(0-24) at any of the investigated dose levels.
译文: 双方性别在任何研究剂量水平下都未显示出Cmax或AUC(0-24)的相关积累。

原文: 	3.	Conclusion
译文: 	3.	结论
--------------------------------------------------
原文: Once daily oral administration (by gavage) of 5, 20 and 80 mg/kg BAY 2469430 to Beagle dogs over a period of 2 weeks was well tolerated without test item-related findings in clinical and postmortem examinations. A decreased thrombocyte count was observed on day 10 in both animals at the high dose of 80 mg/kg.
译文: 每天一次通过灌胃方式给予比格犬 5、20 和 80 mg/kg BAY 2469430 持续 2 周，没有在临床和尸检中发现与测试物相关的结果。第 10 天，在给予高剂量 80 mg/kg 的两只动物中观察到血小板计数下降。
--------------------------------------------------
原文: Toxicokinetic evaluation revealed a dose-dependent increase in exposure, tmax between 1-2 h after administration and no relevant accumulation potential.
译文: 毒代动力学评估显示暴露量随剂量增加，给药后1-2小时内达到峰值（tmax），且没有相关的蓄积潜力。
